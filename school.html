MY School

Vidyamoyee Govt. Girls' High School, located on Rambabu Road in Mymensingh, is a renowned public secondary institution dedicated to female education. Established on January 1, 1873, by Jagatkishore Acharya Chowdhury in honor of his mother, Vidyamoyee Devi, the school has played a pivotal role in advancing women's education in the region.
