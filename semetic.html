VEGETABLES


Vegetables are essential for a healthy diet because they provide a wide range of nutrients, including vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants. Here are some key reasons why vegetables are important:

  1. Rich in Nutrients Vegetables contain essential vitamins (A, C, K, and folate) and minerals (potassium, magnesium, and calcium) that support overall health.

  2. 2. High in Fiber Fiber aids digestion, promotes gut health, and helps prevent constipation. It also supports weight management by keeping you full for longer. 3. Boosts Immunity

  3. Boosts Immunity Many vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, spinach, and broccoli) are rich in vitamin C and antioxidants that help strengthen the immune system.
